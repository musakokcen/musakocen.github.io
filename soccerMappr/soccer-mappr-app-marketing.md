  <section class="sm-section sm-intro">
    <p>Soccer Mappr is a watchOS + iOS app for soccer players who want to understand how they move on the pitch. Strap on your Apple Watch, start a match, and let the app do the rest. When the final whistle blows, your full movement is rendered as a heatmap — right on your wrist, and in full detail on your iPhone.</p>

    <h2>Why Soccer Mappr?</h2>
    <div class="sm-why">
      <div class="sm-why-item">
        <h3>Built for soccer</h3>
        <p>Most fitness apps track running or cycling. Soccer Mappr is designed around the geometry of the pitch and the rhythm of a football match.</p>

      <div class="sm-why-item">

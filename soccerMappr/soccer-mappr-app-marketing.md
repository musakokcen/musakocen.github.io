# Every Match, Mapped.

Soccer Mappr turns your Apple Watch into a personal match tracker. Record your game, see your heatmap, and share your performance — all without leaving the Apple ecosystem.

## About the App

Soccer Mappr is a watchOS + iOS app for soccer players who want to understand how they move on the pitch. Strap on your Apple Watch, start a match, and let the app do the rest. When the final whistle blows, your full movement is rendered as a heatmap — right on your wrist, and in full detail on your iPhone.

**Requires Apple Watch.** Compatible with watchOS 10+ and iOS 17+.

## Key Features

### Live GPS Tracking from Your Wrist

Start your workout directly from Apple Watch. Soccer Mappr records continuous GPS throughout the match, capturing every run, press, and repositioning. The workout syncs automatically to Apple Health.

### Personal Pitch Heatmap

See where you spent the most time on the pitch. The heatmap is plotted from your real GPS route and rendered on a soccer field — so you can instantly spot whether you drifted wide, dominated the middle, or covered the whole park.

### Heatmap Preview on Watch

Before leaving the pitch, get a quick heatmap preview right on your Apple Watch. No need to unlock your phone.

### Full Match Review on iPhone

Every match syncs automatically to your iPhone via CloudKit. Open Soccer Mappr on iPhone to browse your full match history and see a breakdown of each game:

- Distance covered, calories burned, heart rate, duration
- Personal rating, goals, assists, and positions played
- Interactive heatmap in multiple visual styles

### Log Goals, Assists & Positions

After the match, log your stats from your watch: rate your own performance, count your goals and assists, and mark which positions you covered — from GK all the way to striker.

### Multiple Heatmap Styles

Swipe through different visualisations of your movement on the pitch. Classic heat gradients, bold overlays, and more.

### Shareable Heatmaps

Every heatmap style is shareable. Choose whether to include your match stats and health data. Post to Instagram, send to a teammate, or keep a personal archive of your progress.

### Edit Anytime

Forgot to log your assist? No problem. Swipe any match in your history to edit the stats after the fact.

## Why Soccer Mappr?

**Built for soccer.** Most fitness apps track running or cycling. Soccer Mappr is designed specifically around the geometry of the pitch and the rhythm of a football match.

**Your data stays yours.** Health and location data lives in your Apple Health and iCloud. Soccer Mappr never sends it to external servers or shares it with third parties.

**Native Apple experience.** Built entirely with SwiftUI, HealthKit, CloudKit, and MapKit. No third-party SDKs, no ads, no subscriptions.

## Perfect For

- Recreational and competitive soccer players
- Anyone who wants to understand their movement patterns on the pitch
- Players tracking personal stats across a season
- Coaches reviewing player positioning data

## Get Started

Download Soccer Mappr from the App Store, open it on your iPhone, and follow the setup. When you're ready to play, open the app on your Apple Watch and tap **Start Match**.
